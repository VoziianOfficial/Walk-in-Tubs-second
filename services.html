How homeowners often choose

The most useful category usually reflects the real constraint first.

Some homeowners start with installation because the project is new. Others begin with replacement because the current unit no longer feels ideal. In many cases, the most useful difference is whether the real need is repair, accessibility support, or just a clearer planning conversation.

This is why the page is structured around category-first browsing rather than a direct contractor pitch.

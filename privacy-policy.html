Information We Collect

Walk-in Tub Compare may collect information that homeowners choose to submit through forms, quote requests, email communications, or other direct contact methods available on the platform.

This may include information such as name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, selected service category, and project notes related to walk-in tub interests, accessibility needs, bathroom layout, or similar request context.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through the platform may be used to support quote-request pathways, improve user experience, respond to inquiries, maintain platform functionality, and help structure homeowner-provider matching flows.

We may also use information to understand how users interact with categories, legal pages, and navigation elements in order to improve site clarity and usability.

Quote Requests

When homeowners submit quote requests, information may be used as part of the process that helps connect users with independent local service providers or companies that may serve the requested area.

Walk-in Tub Compare is an independent platform and is not a direct installer, contractor, or remodeling company.

Cookies and Similar Technology

The platform may use cookies and similar technologies to support essential functions, remember preference choices, improve navigation experience, and maintain more consistent site performance.

For more details, please review the Cookie Policy.

Information Sharing

Information may be shared where reasonably necessary to support platform operations, user-request routing, quote-request handling, legal compliance, or service-provider connection flows related to the purpose of the site.

We do not describe the platform as directly performing contracted work, and all contractors/providers remain independent.

Data Security

Reasonable efforts may be used to protect submitted information and maintain site integrity. However, no platform, website, or transmission system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

Users may choose whether to submit information through forms, whether to accept or decline cookie preferences where available, and whether to contact the platform by phone or email.
